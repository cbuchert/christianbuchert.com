Email works fine if everyone reads it, someone is willing to play coordinator, and the group has any appetite for threading. Small maker communities tend to run low on all three.

Tool Club is an invite-only app for a group of makers in Springville. Members RSVP to events, propose and vote on ideas, and read recaps. It exposes RSS and iCal feeds so you can stay current without excavating your inbox.

Also an exercise in context engineering: spec and architecture documents written before a line of code, to see how far deliberate context could carry an LLM through a real full-stack build on a tight deadline.
