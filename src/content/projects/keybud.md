---
title: "Keybud"
url: "https://keybud.io/"
subtitle: "A tool to help identify browser keyboard shortcuts."
---

The browser doesn't care what keyboard shortcuts your old desktop app used. Neither does the OS, or the browser running on top of it. Everyone has opinions and none of them consulted each other.

Keybud maps the keyboard across OS and browser combinations so you can see where the collisions are before your users find them for you. Originally built to explain this problem to a skeptical stakeholder. Turned out to be more useful with engineers.
