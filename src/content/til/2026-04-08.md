Your embedded chatbot is probably making your FE bundle significantly bigger because its ability to render any chart you want requires you to ship the whole charting library, instead of treeshaking and bundling just the parts you're using. If the chatbots are going to continue to save us all, it would be smart to find a way to break charting libraries up into pieces that can be downloaded incrementally when responses need them.

This on its own isn't too bad, but figuring out how to do it and also not give the stochastic parrot the ability to sideload and run arbitrary code seems intractable.
